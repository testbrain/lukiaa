Lukiaa Account Deletion

At Lukiaa, we respect your privacy and provide an easy way to delete your account and associated data. Follow the steps below to request account deletion.

How to Delete Your Account

  1. Email us at testbrain.dev@gmail.com from the email address associated with your Lukiaa account.
  2. Include "Account Deletion Request" in the subject line.
  3. Provide your account details (e.g., registered email or username) in the email body.
  4. We will process your request within 30 days and send a confirmation once your account and data are deleted.

If you have any questions or need assistance, please contact our support team at testbrain.dev@gmail.com.
